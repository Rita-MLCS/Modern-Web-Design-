What is a Makerspace?

A makerspace, also known as a hackerspace, is a place where children, youth, and adults engage in exploratory learning and learn through fun and challenging activities.

Makerspaces & hackerspaces are interchangable terms. There is no reference article for makerspace in Wikipedia. When searching for the term makerspace, the reader is redirected to a hackerspace article.

In the reference article , hackerspaces are characterized as "a community-operated, often Not For Profit" (501(c)(3) in the United States), workspace where

people with common interests, often in computers, machining, technology, science, digital art or electronic art, can meet, socialize and collaborate."
